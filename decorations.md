# History of visual decorations for Lisp

- 2004 - [S-expression boxes]
- 2005 - [Scratch], not actually sure if close to lisp
- 2010 - [Rainbow delimiters]
- 2010 - [Scheme bricks]
- 2013 - [indent-guide.el]
- 2015 - [Frame-based editing], not lisp
- 2015 - [Plastic]
- 2016 - [Cirru]
